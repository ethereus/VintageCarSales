BMW E23

The BMW E23 is the first generation of the BMW 7 Series luxury cars and was produced from 1977 to 1986. It was built in a 4-door sedan body style with 6-cylinder engines, to replace the BMW 'New Six' (E3) sedans. From 1983 to 1986, a turbocharged 6-cylinder engine was available.

The E23 was produced for ten years from 1977 to 1987. The L7 continued to be offered in the United States for another year even though the last E23 built in Germany was produced in August 1986.
